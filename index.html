5 Steps to become Web Developer
  • List of Couses (current)
  • In Depth Learning
Start studying programming

Start studying courses on Web Developement in English. Below you can find recommended list of courses to take. Post all the meaningful code, that you are going to write during your studies, to github. Apply gained skills to write your own pet projects and post it to github also.

Create a page on LinekedIn

Create a page on LinekedIn and write there, that you are JS developer freelancer, when you starting your studies.

Prepare a CV

Prepare a CV, where you can write eveything that you have learned on courses as an experience and write in Skills, all the tools that you have used during your studies. And when you feeling ready to get a job post it online and send to the positions, that you are interested in. You might even want to find someone, who works at that company on LinkedIn and ask them to refer you there.

Fail interviews and study more

Fail on few first interview and make a list of things that you couldn't answer during the interview. You can use some simple site as this one, to track your interwiew questions and read questions, that other had on their interviews.

Get you first development job!

Get you first development job! And keep studying advanced materials to know Algorithms and Design Patterns to be able to land you Dream Job at the company of your Dreams.
